# Simply Routine — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Simply Routine is a personal scheduling app designed to help you manage your daily routine.

## Data we collect

By default, Simply Routine collects no personal data. Everything you enter —
events, tasks, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device
and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

### Household sync (optional)

If you choose to enable household sharing, Simply Routine uses Google Firebase
to sync shared task names and completion dates between devices you have
explicitly paired with. This data is:

- Only synced when you opt in by setting up a household
- Only accessible to devices you have deliberately paired with
- Stored on Google Firebase servers, subject to
  [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Never accessed by us

You can stop syncing at any time by removing paired devices in Settings.
Unpaired devices return to fully local storage.

## Third-party services

Simply Routine does not use analytics, advertising, or crash reporting.

When household sync is enabled, the app uses:

- **Google Firebase** — for syncing shared tasks between paired devices
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** — to notify paired devices of task updates

The app is distributed via Google Play. Google's own privacy policy governs
any data collected as part of that distribution.

## Permissions

Simply Routine requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications** — to display your current event and upcoming reminders
- **Boot on startup** — to restore your event reminders after your device restarts
- **Exact alarms** — to deliver event alerts at the correct time
- **Internet access** — used only when household sync is enabled, to sync
  shared tasks between paired devices
