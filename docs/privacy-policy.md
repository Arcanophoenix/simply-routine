# Simply Routine — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

Simply Routine is a personal scheduling app designed to help you manage your daily routine.

## Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Everything you enter into Simply Routine — events, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Third-party services

Simply Routine does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

The app is distributed via Google Play. Google's own privacy policy governs any data collected as part of that distribution.

## Permissions

Simply Routine requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications** — to display your current event and upcoming reminders
- **Boot on startup** — to restore your event reminders after your device restarts
- **Exact alarms** — to deliver event alerts at the correct time

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.
